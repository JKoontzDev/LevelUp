Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 14, 2025

1. Introduction

LevelUp is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our gamified self-improvement app.

We may collect the following types of information:

We use your information to:

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties period!

5. User Responsibilities

You agree not to access, modify, or share another user’s data without permission. Unauthorized access to others’ data is a violation of this Policy and our Terms of Service.

7. Your Choices

You can:

  • Update or delete your account data through the App’s settings.

8. Children’s Privacy

LevelUp is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we learn such data has been collected, we will delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via discord or in-app notifications.
